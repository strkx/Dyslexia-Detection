Understanding Dyslexia Better

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning disorder that involves difficulty reading due to problems identifying speech sounds and learning how they relate to letters and words.

Common Signs

Signs include delayed reading ability, difficulty spelling, trouble following instructions, and challenges with sequence organization.

Support Available

Various support methods include specialized education programs, assistive technology, and accommodations in school and workplace settings.
