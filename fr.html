Understanding and Supporting Dyslexia

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning disorder that affects reading, writing, and spelling. It is not related to intelligence but rather how the brain processes language. People with dyslexia often have difficulty with phonological processing, which makes it hard to decode words.

How Can We Help?

Early identification and intervention are key to supporting individuals with dyslexia. Our website provides resources, tools, and a cognitive test to help you understand dyslexia better.
